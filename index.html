Crystal Collector

Instructions

You are given a random number at the start of the game. You'll have 4 crystals below. Clicking on the crystals will give you a set amount of points. You win by getting the exact random number needed. The value of each crystal is hidden until until you click on it. Each time the game restarts the value's will not be the same.
